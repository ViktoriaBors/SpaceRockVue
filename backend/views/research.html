The Research Facility and its services

The research facility is fully equipped with instruments, which are necessary for sample characterization.

The laboratory main focus is to determine the geochemical and mineral composition of the analogue samples and simulants. We also have a numerous sample preparation equipment for example rock crusher, saw, miller etc.

Our laboratory park includes the following instruments:

  • Polarized Light Microscope (PLM).
  • X-ray Powder Diffraction (XRD).
  • X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometer (XRF).
  • Fourier -transform Infrared Spectroscope (FTIR).
  • Raman Spectroscope.
  • Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM-EDS).

Please NOTE that our services are not available for commercial use. We characterize only the in-house samples and samples which is waiting to be approved.
